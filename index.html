The Doctors

In 2016, Dr. Anthony Craig associated with Dr. S. Todd Comm, who had been practicing in Dixon since 1979. In 2017, Dr. Allison Craig began practicing here as well.

In 2021, after over 40 years of service, Dr. Comm retired. The Drs. Craig are committed to preserving the small, family dental practice the community of Dixon has grown to love.

Our Practice

We first opened our doors in Dixon in 1979, and moved to our current location in 1990. Our patients appreciate the large windows in the operatories, and the cozy feel of the office.

Your First Visit

Going to the dentist does not have to be a scary experience. Our goal is to make you feel comfortable and safe.

We accept all dental PPO plans, and process insurance claims on behalf of our patients.

The cost of dental care does not have to be a burden. We value personal relationships with patients, and offer flexible payment plans.
